First evening

Rest, then a gentle stroll

Check in, freshen up, and keep it light — tomorrow starts before dawn. A slow riverside walk and dinner in your quartier is the perfect welcome.

  • Pick up a Navigo Easy card each (€2) and load ticket-books — €2.55 a ride, valid on metro, RER, bus & the Montmartre funicular.
  • Lay out photoshoot outfits tonight; set an early alarm once the shoot time is confirmed.
  • Early night — catching that first soft light tomorrow is the whole magic.

The traveller's essentials

Getting around

Metro, on foot, by taxi

  • Navigo Easy card (€2) loaded with tickets — €2.55 metro/RER/train, €2.05 bus. One card can't be shared across turnstiles, so one each.
  • Central Paris is compact — most days here are walkable loops. The metro is for the longer hops (Eiffel→Arc, Montmartre→Cité).
  • The Montmartre funicular takes a normal metro ticket — no separate fare.
  • Taxis & Uber are easy; keep the airport flat fares in mind for the return.
Money & markets

Cards everywhere, a little cash

  • Contactless cards & phones work almost everywhere. Carry ~€40 cash for falafel, market stalls and tips.
  • Restaurant service is included — round up or leave a euro or two for good service.
  • Tap water is free and safe — ask for une carafe d'eau.
Staying comfortable

Gentle on the feet

  • Lifts: Eiffel (to summit) and the Montmartre funicular avoid the stairs. The Arc has a lift for reduced mobility — ask staff. A boon if anyone in the party tires easily.
  • Rest stops: Tuileries' green chairs by the fountains and Place des Vosges arcades are made for pausing.
  • Cobbles in Montmartre & the Marais — cushioned, closed shoes beat sandals.
  • Carry water; late-July afternoons hit the mid-20s.
Stay sharp

Petty theft, simply avoided

  • Pickpockets work Trocadéro, the Eiffel lawns, metro Line 1, the Champs and Montmartre steps. Bags zipped & in front.
  • Ignore the "gold ring" and friendship-bracelet approaches — polite non merci and keep walking.
  • Phones out of back pockets on the metro.
